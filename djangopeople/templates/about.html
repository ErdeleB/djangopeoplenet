Why are we doing it?

KungfuPeople is just for fun - we're not asking you for any money, and we won't be selling your data or information to other companies. We're martial artists, and we want to help promote kung fu simply because we think the world would be a better place if everyone was training. We all know the potential of the internet for spreading information and connecting people, so this is our way of helping.

Ulterior motive

We have only one ulterior motive - promoting kung fu! We're not tied to any particular club or organisation, but we hope that this website can help kung fu clubs to grow and spread their message, and attract more and more people to train kung fu. Read here how you can use this website to promote your club.
