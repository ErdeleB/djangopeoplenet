Discover your true potential with Kamon.
A Wing Chun system that’s easy to learn and guaranteed to get results fast.

Our mission is to teach progressive Wing Chun kung fu for self defence, to improve self-confidence and to inspire and encourage a sense of lifelong personal development and learning.

What is Wing Chun Kung Fu? Wing Chun Kung Fu is a close quarter Chinese fighting system first seen in the west demonstrated by movie star legend Bruce Lee. It was brought from China to Hong Kong in 1949 by Bruce Lee’s teacher, Grandmaster Ip Man (aka Yip man, see Ip Man movie trailer).

Grandmaster Ip Man playing Chi Sao with Bruce Lee          Donnie Yen in Ip Man Movie

Wing Chun was created by Shaolin nun, Ng Mui, in the 17th century and named after her first student, Yim Wing Chun. It has been passed on and developed by successive masters to become what it is today, a unique fighting system for personal self defence.

What is Kamon Wing Chun? Kamon Wing Chun was founded by Master Kevin Chan, who is a life member of the Yip Man Martial Arts Association. Kamon Wing Chun is built on the solid roots of Ip Man Wing Chun, and has developed to reflect the self defence needs and lifestyle of the today’s men and women. Through regular weekly training you will gain self defence skills as well as make new friends, improve self confidence and fitness and relieve the stress of today’s hectic world.

Kevin Chan (centre left picture) assisting students, and a typical class (right) at Pineapple Dance Studio Covent Garden, London branch.

Why train with us? Kamon Wing Chun caters for all ages, fitness levels, build, gender and natural ability. We do not just cater for the young athletic type (although they are most welcome), we genuinely want to help all people in a supportive environment. We will help you learn at your pace, whether you are a natural or require extra time and attention. We are there to develop you.

We do not waste time or cut corners when teaching you authentic Wing Chun. Time is precious. Your progress to learn effectively matters to us.

Kamon Wing Chun is neither a fight club nor a group of people learning to be street fighters. Our members are balanced individuals who enjoy health and fitness through an exciting combat form of martial art.

Rather than going to the gym and working on the same old machines, Wing Chun offers a challenging way of getting fit.

The Benefits of Training with Kamon:
  • Become proficient in an established fighting system
  • Improved health and fitness
  • Boost your self confidence & self-awareness
  • Improved balance & co-ordination
  • Train in a safe, friendly & positive environment
  • Beginners welcome, classes cater for all levels
